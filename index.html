Upload an Excel file to see options analysis

1. Upload Files
You upload your Excel file twice a day (morning & evening). The system reads date and session (I/II) from the filename automatically (e.g. 20_FEB_II.xlsx). Data is instantly shared with all friends.
2. NET Score
NET = FutLong − FutShort + CallLong − CallShort − PutLong + PutShort
Positive NET = retail is net LONG. Negative = net SHORT.
3. Z-Score (Core Signal)
Z = (Today's NET − Historical Average) ÷ Std Deviation

Z > +1.5 = Retail extremely crowded LONG → your contra is SELL
Z < −1.5 = Retail extremely crowded SHORT → your contra is BUY
4. Trend Confirmation
If retail builds longs for 3+ consecutive sessions AND Z-Score is extreme = STRONG SELL. More data uploaded = higher confidence signals.
